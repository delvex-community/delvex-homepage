ABOUT THE COMMUNITY

The Delvex Community is derived and defined by our unique culture. A trusted online community, that is a place to learn and share the technical knowledge to build an ecosystem for accelerating open technology development.

  • Founded in 2020, Delvex's mission is to enable mass innovation through open source software stack and contributions by bringing the technology lovers closer together.
  • Our community creates a platform to learn, share and provides the quick and clever solution ( tool / package / codebase / automated solution ) to the real world standard-technology problems or the problems which the developers regularly experience including GSOC problems etc.
  • So next time if you have any questions, Delvex Community will be the place to look out for for their solutions and the best practices.
  • Delvex also delivers impactful training conferences and events to discover and share what's new going on in the technical world.
  • We encourage people to be bold, skeptical towards problems and to be able to solve the ones they care about the most.
  • Open source contribution: Delvex makes it easy to comprehend and gives a deep insight to open source. Consider Delvex before “actually” contributing to the open source softwares. Here, you get the chance to be part of an active open source community where you can meet like-minded people.
